HISTORY

2000

Established with the mission of creating satisfying digital solutions for management issues of government agencies, municipalities and the private sector; presenting its first MIS (Management Information Systems) solution.

2001

The first web-based MIS that had an SQL database in Turkey is developed for municipalities, addressing the need for an inclusive, user-friendly management system.

2008

GIS-based desktop CAD software specialized in city planning and map servers put in place for clients.

2012

The R&D project of GIS library which was funded by The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey is completed successfully to support customized software projects of Belsis clients in an effective way.

2014

Invested in two technopolises for new branches of R&D and GIS development.

2014

Electronic Document Management (EDMS) system developed and integrated into MIS solutions to serve the public and private sectors.

2016

MobileCAD and MobileGIS developed and started to be integrated and used in both MIS and customized vertical software solutions.

2019

Spatial Business Intelligence developed to increase the efficiency of data-driven decision-making processes.

2023

We are continuously contributing to MIS and GIS community.
